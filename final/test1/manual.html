How to set chords

You may play this guitar without any manual if you like, but if you need to use the pre-set chords:

Press Key on Keyboard:

  1. Chord C: Press C
  2. Chord G: Press G
  3. Chord Am: Press A
  4. Chord F: Press F
  5. Chord D7: Press D
  6. Chord Em: Press E
  7. clear Chord: Press TAB
